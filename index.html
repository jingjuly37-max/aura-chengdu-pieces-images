BOTANICAL REVERIE

Nature Captured — Wearable Art from Chengdu’s Gardens

Born from the quiet gardens and slow afternoons of Chengdu, Botanical Reverie transforms the city’s ephemeral beauty into permanent wearable art. Each piece begins with real botanicals—handpicked petals and leaves native to Sichuan—carefully preserved within crystal-clear resin and precious metal settings. Crafted as limited releases, these pieces are a poetic ode to slow living.

VINTAGE WHISPER

The Heirloom Statement — Where East Meets West in Chengdu

Inspired by Chengdu’s unique blend of old-world charm and cosmopolitan elegance, Vintage Whisper reinterprets grandeur for the contemporary connoisseur. Drawing from both European archival design and Chinese artisanal techniques, each piece is handcrafted using textured metals, antique-finish brass, and luminous freshwater pearls. Conceived as modern heirlooms, these works are more than mere adornment.
